Section 2

These videos are designed to be used in conjunction with the developer's guide and applications (WAApplication01.nsf and WAApplication02.nsf) provided here. The videos demonstrate what the various mechanisms look like in operation to the end-user while the applications provide hands-on experience in using them. The guide illustrates (in most cases graphically) the components which make up those mechanisms and (hopefully) serves as an ongoing reference in developing workflow applications.
