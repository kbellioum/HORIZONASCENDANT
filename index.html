    The Need

    Current BPM development tools and practices are severely limited by the lack of a coherent methodology implemented in the context of a robust platform. In the absence of such a framework, companies are unable to cope with the challenges of developing, reusing and maintaining software solutions in an ever evolving environment over the long term. Adding to the challenge is the need to bring that framework within the reach of power business users while simultaneously providing the adaptability to implement highly complex business requirements by IT professionals.

    The Solution

    Enter Workflow Ascendant. Document driven design powers this low-code development framework. These documents allow the expression of highly adaptable routing rules and code abstraction (where references to application specific modules are associated with workflow document events). This approach provides the ability for power business users to express business logic while simultaneously providing the option to include complex functionality by IT professionals. This framework runs on the IBM Domino platform, the one infrastructure furnishing the unmatched durability, scalability and interconnectivity required to meet todayâ€™s enterprise business challenges.
